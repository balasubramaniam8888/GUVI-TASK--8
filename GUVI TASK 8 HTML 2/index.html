Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of bangaladesh

Rebel Poet

Karzi nazrul islam is the national poet of bangladesh who is especially recognized as a bidrohi kobi(rbel poet)around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philosopher of bangaladesh.The famous poet nazrul's writings deal with the revolution of that period of the british goverment.His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of bangladesh.He has raised his voice aganist any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity around the indian subcontinent.



Awesome Writter

Azrul's writtings explored themes such as love,freedom,humanity,and revolution.He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism,including reglious,caste-based and gender-based.Throughout his career,nazrul wrote short stories,novels,and essays but is best known for his songs and poems,he pioneered new music forms such as bengali ghazals,nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on hmv and gramophone records),collectively known as nazrul geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount."-Kazi Nazrul Islam
